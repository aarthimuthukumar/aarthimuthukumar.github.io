Teens Tackle Tobacco: Drug Usage Prevention Conference

I work at the Alameda County Department of Education as the Chairwoman of Outreach and Communitcations on their Youth Advisory Board. As Chairwoman, I am in charge of working with the other members of the board to plan a conference at the University of California at Berkeley each year. This conference focuses on educating teens on drug and tobacco usage; however, it also addresses themes of gender equality and support for the LGBTQ+ community. At the conference, for the past two years, I have spoken on gender equality and how it is imperative for the future of the world.

I am incredibly passionate about gender equality, especially since I am a Woman in STEM. I am an advocate for gender equality, and hope to work in a related field when I enter the workforce.
